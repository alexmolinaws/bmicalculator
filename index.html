What is body mass index?

Are you curious about your body mass index (BMI) and what it means for your health? Our test offers a quick and easy way to calculate this standart measurement that helps to assess your body weight by providing a general indication of whether you're underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese, which can be useful for understanding potential health risks associated with weight.
