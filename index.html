What is the body mass index?

This is a free BMI calculator app. Are you curious about your body mass index (BMI) and what it means for your health? This user-friendly app offers a quick and easy way to calculate this standarized measurement that helps to assess your body weight relative to your height. It provides a general indication of whether you're underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese, which can be useful for understanding potential health risks associated with weight.
